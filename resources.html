foo_vis_milk2 Resources

foo_vis_milk2 uses .milk preset files and texture images to generate visuals. The component creates the milkdrop2\presets\ and milkdrop2\textures\ directories automatically under your foobar2000 profile.

Getting Presets

projectM Presets

The projectM project maintains a large collection of MilkDrop-compatible presets:

  • projectM preset packs

MilkDrop 3

The MilkDrop 3 project includes presets and is another source of MilkDrop content.

BeatDrop

Likewise, the BeatDrop project maintains a collection of MilkDrop presets and textures.

Community Presets

The MilkDrop preset community has been active for over 20 years. Additional presets can be found at:

  • Various preset packs shared on forums and social media by preset authors

Getting Textures

Presets that use texture mixing need texture files in milkdrop2\textures\. Many preset packs include the standard texture set.

foo_vis_milk2 supports:

  • JPEG, PNG, BMP, TGA, DDS texture formats
  • See the Textures Guide for details on texture loading and configuration
